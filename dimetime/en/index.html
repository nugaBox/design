Synopsis

A world of chaos where no trace of life can be found.

"Time" flowed from the clock tower,
which was generated by the confluence of light and shadow,
as a beam of light rushes in and makes a shadow.
The sun rose brightly in the sky,
and green grass sprung on the desolate terrain.
Soon after, trees grew and forests developed,
as birdsong softly echoed forth.
Finally, the sun's children were born.
Their ruby eyes shone brighter than the warm sunlight.
The children dubbed the clock tower,
which had stood since before they were born, the "Tower of DimeTime".
Its inherent brightness is said to be seen from everywhere on the planet.

Even the light, however, did not last forever.
The Tower of DimeTime became worn-out, shattered,
and is no longer capable of capturing time.
All living creatures fell asleep in a world where time had stopped
and the shadow of chaos had been cast,
yet the will of light remained in the children's ruby eyes.

The children decide to find the dispersed bits
of Tower of DimeTime in order to turn back time.
The search for the elusive PoT(Pieces of Time) began.
